High School Math Relays

The 14th Annual PREP-KC Regional High School Math Relays will be held at Ruskin High School from 8:30 am - 3pm on Nov 11th, 2023.

Participants will compete in one individual, multi-round event covering content from various math topics and one team competition that consists of various problem-solving events.

There are levels to it

Math Benchmarking

Students will be placed in individual competitions based on their enrolled fall semester math course. Team competitions will consist of mixed groups (ages and course levels).

Fame and Fortune

Scholarship for Sigma

Awards are provided to the top five individual winners from each level of the competition. A $500 scholarship, awarded to the winner of the Sigma division of the high school competition.

Awards

The top four students in each event will be awarded medals and a certificate. The school that scores the most points will receive the math relays champion traveling trophy. The district that receives the highest percentage of points based on number of participants will also receive a trophy. The winners are determined by a calculation of points earned in each competition.
